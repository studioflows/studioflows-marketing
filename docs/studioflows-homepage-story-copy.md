## Story spine

A founder-led service business looks fine from the outside. Underneath, everything still routes through one person. The page names that dependency, breaks the illusion, reframes AI and software, introduces StudioFlows as the layer that runs the work, proves it with a Friday-night scenario, shows how the system learns and earns trust, then turns from darkness into light and opens two paths forward.
